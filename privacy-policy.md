# FTC Scouting App – Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2025

Thank you for using the **FTC Scouting App**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

---

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect
The FTC Scouting App **does not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

We do not collect:
- Names  
- Email addresses  
- Phone numbers  
- Location data  
- Contacts  
- Photos or media  
- Usage analytics  
- Device identifiers  
- Any other personal data  

The app works entirely **without user accounts** and does not connect to external servers.

---

## 2. Information Stored on Your Device
The app may store **non-personal scouting data locally**, such as:
- Match notes  
- Team performance data  
- Statistics you enter  

This data:
- Stays **on your device only**  
- Is **not transmitted** to any server  
- Is **not shared** unless you manually export it  

---

## 3. How We Use Data
Any data you enter is used **only for scouting and strategy during FTC competitions**.

We do **not**:
- Transmit data externally  
- Share data with third parties  
- Use data for ads  
- Track user behavior  

---

## 4. Data Sharing
We **do not share any data** with third parties.  
All scouting data remains private unless you manually share it.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy
The app is safe for all ages, including children under 13.  
Since we collect **no personal information**, the app is compliant with **COPPA** guidelines.

---

## 6. Security
Because no personal data is collected or stored externally, your private information is never at risk.  
Any exported files are your responsibility to protect.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy as needed. Updates will be posted on this page.
